About Our Hotel

The History Of Our Hotel

Tiennara is more than just a luxurious getaway; it is a place steeped in history and cultural significance. The name "Tiennara" is derived from the ancient term for "Heavenly Shore," reflecting the idyllic and serene nature of the location. Originally, Tien Beach was a hidden gem known only to locals and a few intrepid travelers. The area was historically a fishing village, with a community that lived in harmony with the sea. Over time, as word of its beauty spread, Tien Beach became a coveted destination for those seeking a peaceful retreat.

The Sunset Bar at Tiennara is the ideal spot to unwind and enjoy breathtaking views as the sun dips below the horizon. The Swan Farm

Tiennara's Swan Farm offers a unique and enchanting experience for visitors. Home to graceful swans and other local wildlife, the farm provides an opportunity to connect with nature in a tranquil setting. Guests can enjoy peaceful walks around the pond, feed the swans, and learn about the farm's efforts in wildlife conservation and sustainable practices.
