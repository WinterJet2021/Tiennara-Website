About Tiennara Resort

Welcome to Tiennara, your ultimate private staycation destination. Nestled along the serene shores of Tien Beach, Tiennara offers exclusive access to over 180 meters of pristine, private beachfront. This luxurious retreat is designed for those who seek tranquility, beauty, and unparalleled comfort.

Our elegantly appointed suites and villas provide the perfect blend of luxury and homeliness. Each accommodation offers breathtaking ocean views and is equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable and memorable stay. Choose from a variety of options, including beachfront villas, spacious suites, and cozy bungalows, each designed to cater to your unique needs.
